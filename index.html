About the Nepal Police Technical ASI Exam

The Nepal Police Technical Police Assistant Inspector (IT/Computer) exam is a competitive examination that tests candidates on their theoretical knowledge, practical skills, physical fitness, and interview performance.

The exam consists of:
  • Written MCQ test with 20% negative marking
  • Practical exam testing IT skills
  • Health checks (height, weight, eyesight)
  • Interview

Our preparation plan is designed to help you excel in all components of this challenging exam.

Exam Components

Written Exam 200 marks
Practical Exam 50 marks
Health Checks Pass/Fail
Interview 30 marks
