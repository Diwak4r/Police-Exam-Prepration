Comprehensive Free Resources for Nepal Police Technical ASI Exam

This curated collection of free resources will support your preparation for all components of the Nepal Police Technical ASI exam. These resources have been carefully selected to complement your 12-week preparation plan and help strengthen your theoretical knowledge, practical skills, physical fitness, and interview readiness.

How to Use These Resources

For maximum benefit, integrate these resources into your daily study schedule:

  • Focus on resources that address your specific weak areas
  • Allocate dedicated time for each resource type in your daily schedule
  • Track your progress through courses and tutorials
  • Apply what you learn through practice exercises and mock tests
  • Bookmark this page for easy reference throughout your preparation

All resources listed are completely free to access and use.
